Scriptures About Jesus Christ

A small project website where I collect scriptures that testify of the Savior.

About This Project

This website is part of my personal project for studying the scriptures. I chose several passages that teach me who Jesus Christ is, what He has done for me, and how I can come unto Him. By building this website, I spent more time reading, thinking, and writing about the Savior instead of just scrolling through the verses quickly.

Each page focuses on one scripture. I first share the verse, then explain what it teaches about Christ, and finally write how I can apply it in my everyday life. In this way, the project becomes more than just a technical website assignment—it becomes a way for me to bear testimony of Jesus Christ and record my personal thoughts.

What I Hope to Learn

As I continue to study, I hope to notice new details in these verses: words that describe Christ’s character, promises that He gives, and invitations He extends. I also hope that anyone who visits this website can feel a little more of His love and be reminded to look to Him for strength and peace.
